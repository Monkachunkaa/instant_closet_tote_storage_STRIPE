Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our storage service

We deliver empty totes directly to your door (minimum 2 totes, no maximum). Once you've filled them with your items, we pick them up and store them in our secure facility. When you need your items back, we deliver them right to your door again.

No trucks needed! That's the beauty of our service. We handle all transportation with our delivery vehicles. You don't need to worry about renting trucks, making trips to storage facilities, or fitting large items in your car. If you prefer, you can also drop your totes off yourself at our facility to avoid the delivery fee and save significantly compared to renting an entire storage locker.

No lifting required on your part! Our team handles all the heavy lifting during pickup and delivery. You simply pack your items into the totes at your convenience, and we take care of the rest. No hurt backs or strain!

We provide metal security seals for each side of every tote. Once you've packed your items and are ready for pickup, you seal both sides of the tote yourself. Each seal has a private identifier to ensure your totes are never mixed up with others. This ensures your tote is never opened at any point during storage, and all your items remain fully secured and tamper-proof.

Our storage totes are 27-gallon capacity, durable, and weather-resistant. They're perfect for storing seasonal items, holiday decorations, clothing, keepsakes, and other household items. Each tote is designed to protect your belongings during transport and storage.

The minimum is 2 totes to get started, but there's no maximum limit! Whether you need 2 totes or 20, we can accommodate your storage needs. Our pricing is simple at $10 per tote per month.

Our pricing is simple and transparent: $10 per tote per month for ongoing storage. Getting started costs $40 minimum (2 totes) which includes your first month of storage plus your first two $10 trip fees.

You can store most household items including seasonal decorations, clothing, keepsakes, books, small furniture pieces, and personal belongings. Items should fit within the 27-gallon tote capacity. Please contact us if you have questions about specific items.
